Bolognese

This homemade Bolognese sauce is dedicated to the late great Marcella Hazan.

She was considered the Julia Child of Italian food. At a time when most

Americans thought Bolognese was spaghetti sauce with chunks of hamburger,

Marcella taught us just how magnificent this meat sauce could be. I like to toss it

with some mezzi rigatoni and serve it with a little grated Parmesan cheese and a

sprinkle of parsley.
